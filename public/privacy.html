Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy outlines how Dependable Painting collects, uses, and protects any information you provide when using this website.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information through our contact forms or via browser cookies:

  • Contact information including your name, phone number, and email address.
  • Project details such as service location and a description of the work needed.
  • Anonymous website usage data through analytics tools to help us improve user experience.

How We Use This Information

We collect this data for the sole purpose of conducting our business and providing you with the best possible service. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide accurate service estimates.
  • Schedule and manage painting projects.
  • Improve our services and the functionality of our website.
  • Internally track marketing and service effectiveness.

Data Security

We are committed to keeping your information secure. We utilize industry-standard security measures, including HTTPS encryption and secure server infrastructure, to prevent unauthorized access or disclosure of your data.

Cookies and Analytics

Our website uses cookies to enhance functionality. We also use third-party analytics services, such as Google Analytics, to gather anonymous data about website traffic. This helps us understand user behavior and improve our site. These services may place their own cookies on your browser.

Your Consent

By using our website and submitting your information, you consent to this privacy policy.
