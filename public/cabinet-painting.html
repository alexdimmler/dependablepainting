The Modern Way to Update Your Kitchen

Our Spray-Finish Process

We achieve a perfect, brush-stroke-free finish by following a rigorous process:

  • Removal & Labeling: Doors and drawers are carefully removed and labeled.
  • Degreasing & Cleaning: All surfaces are deep-cleaned to remove contaminants.
  • Sanding & Priming: We sand and apply a high-adhesion primer for a strong bond.
  • Spraying: Multiple coats of durable cabinet enamel are sprayed for a smooth finish.
  • Reassembly: We professionally reinstall everything for a perfect fit.

Cabinet Painting FAQs

How durable is painted cabinet finish?

How durable is painted cabinet finish?

Extremely durable. We don't use wall paint. We use a high-performance cabinet enamel that cures to a hard, furniture-grade finish, designed to withstand daily use in kitchens and bathrooms.

How long does the cabinet painting process take?

How long does the cabinet painting process take?

The average kitchen project takes about 4-5 days from start to finish. This includes on-site prep of the cabinet boxes and off-site finishing of the doors and drawers.

Will my kitchen be usable during the process?

Will my kitchen be usable during the process?

Yes, for the most part. While the doors and drawers are off, your kitchen remains functional. We mask and protect all surrounding surfaces, but you can still access your countertops and appliances in the evenings.

Can you paint any type of cabinet?

Can you paint any type of cabinet?

We can paint most cabinet types, including solid wood, MDF, and previously painted or stained cabinets. We cannot paint thermofoil or laminate cabinets, as the paint will not adhere properly for a lasting finish.
